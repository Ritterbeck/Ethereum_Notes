ERC-20  -> Fungible tokens
ERC-721 -> Non-fungible tokens

\begin{definition}[Fungible]
% https://www.merriam-webster.com/thesaurus/fungible
capable of being substituted in place of one another
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Fungible Token]
% My definition
a token that is fungible
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Non-Fungible Token]
% My definition
a token that is not fungible.
\end{definition}
